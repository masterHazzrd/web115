Erik the Red

First European to land on and settle in Greenland

Here's a time line of Erik the Red's life

  • 950 CE - Erik the Red was born Erik Thorvaldsson to Thorvald Asvaldsson in Jaeren, Rogaland, Norway.
  • 982 CE - Erik was exiled for killing his neighbour, Eyiolf the Foul. He left Iceland and sailed west with his family.
    He found the land previously discovered by Gunnbjorn Ulfsson. He sailed along the coast until he found an area free
    from ice and settled there for his period of exile
